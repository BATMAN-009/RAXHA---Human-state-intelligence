## Freeze policy
- **Status: Academy v1.0 — pending the Competency-15 gate close.** The 15-competency core arc is materially complete on disk.
- **On Competency-15 close → Academy v1.0 is FROZEN — as an RFC 1.0, not as "finished":** **immutable, versioned, historically preserved.** Improvements become numbered RFCs (`RFC-001`, `RFC-002`, …) that *supersede* — never "I edited the old chapter." The reasoning history is preserved because it is often as valuable as the final decision.
- **The Doctrine and Canon are the authority** the `engineering/` tier derives from (via `../../academy/…` links). They are part of the frozen foundation (`engineering/00-foundation/00A-FOUNDATION-LOCK.md`).
