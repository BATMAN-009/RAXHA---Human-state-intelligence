### AQ-3 — "Knowledge Graph Connections" section §22 (proposed 2026-07-13)
Add §22 to every chapter from Competency 6 onward (retrofit earlier chapters opportunistically). Directly serves the "Human State Intelligence System" mission (each node = a piece of human-state evidence with dependencies + confidence).
**Status:** ✅ **RATIFIED 2026-07-13** (Competency 6 gate passed). §22 "Knowledge Graph Connections" now a standing chapter section (template = §1–22).
